Fold-it-yourself reception pieces

Elegant on the table. Yours to fold.

Each set includes printed crane sheets with fold guides and gold-accented patterns. Take a calm moment to fold them yourself, then tuck in your guest cards for a reception detail that feels personal, considered, and beautifully handmade.

Not pre-folded These cranes do not arrive assembled. Each piece comes as a flat, printed sheet — part of the charm is the simple fold that turns paper into a place card holder at your own pace.
What arrives Flat printed sheets with fold lines, gold botanical and geometric accents, and room for your guest names.
The fold A guided origami fold — straightforward, satisfying, and meant to be part of the preparation.
On the table Finished cranes hold escort cards, place cards, table names, menus, or small signs with quiet ceremony.
Style Japanese-inspired, formal, modern, and understated in matte black, ivory, and gold.
